What is it?

Overfishing all over the globe has turned the practice into a national issue that affects our natural ecosystem as well as the well-being of everyone on this planet. Every year, almost 25% of of fish stocks are depleted and/or overexploited, while another 52% remains in imminent danger. A lack of stringent federal regulations and illegal fishing has contributed terribly to the amassing destruction, and not enough awareness is being brought to this issue. This game has been designed to give kids ages 5+ a depiction of the true state of human affairs if overfishing continues at the rate it occurs with today. Recently, after the advent of Pokemon Go, there has been a slight resurgence in the use of retro Pokemon games such as Fire Red. In an attempt to reach younger audiences, play off the hype, and convey an important message, we have based this webgame off the style of these games, whilst adding Google API's, Artificial Intelligence, and modern day issues to spice up the proceedings. Balance your fishing just right to maintain a healthy ecosystem. Else, the whole world will come crashing down... Best of Luck!!
